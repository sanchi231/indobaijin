Infrastructure

  • IBJ is a joint venture with world leaders in CS2 technology - Shanghai Baijin, China, which will ensure continuous up-gradation of technology
  • It is a modern automated plant which ensures safe and quality product with the capacity of 75,000 MTPA. We are the leading manufacturer of Carbon Disulphide (CS2) in India with largest storage capacity of 6500 MT
  • Our CS2 manufacturing plant is the first of its kind in India to use Green Technology. While, most of the existing CS2 plants in India are based on the old charcoal-based technology, which has adverse impact on the environment
  • By adopting green technology, deforestation of 65 SQ kms per annum is being avoided by our plant,
  • Maintaining Zero Discharge plant status, by implementing 3 R Concept
  • Sulphur recovery efficiency is in excess of 99.9 %, highest achieved in India till date in such plants
  • Our plant is one of the safest plants, due to latest technology, innovative approaches and employee involvement in best safety practices
  • IBJ ensures all its operations through qualified & trained manpower & from centralized sophisticated DCS Control Room
